Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift underserved communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and opportunities that enable individuals to lead dignified and self-reliant lives.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual has equal access to basic needs, opportunities, and the ability to shape their own future.

Who We Are

About Us

RajKriti Foundations is a non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare and community development. Founded on the belief that collective action can drive meaningful and lasting change, we aim to empower individuals and uplift communities through inclusive and sustainable initiatives. Our work focuses on creating opportunities that promote growth, dignity, and self-reliance.

We collaborate closely with local communities to understand their needs and address critical challenges such as education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and social equity. Through the support of volunteers, partners, and well-wishers, we strive to build a more compassionate and equitable society, making a lasting impact on the lives we serve.

Nourishing Lives, Preventing Waste
